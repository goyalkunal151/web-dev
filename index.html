DANCE CLUB

About Us

Cadence- the word itself alludes rhythm and the artistic flow of beats. Dance is an art, a creative portal, a form of expression and an ever evolving form of art. As the choreography club the aim is to promote the culture of these evolving dance styles ranging from hip hop to classical !!!

What we do!

Organising various dance events within the campus and participate in competitions outside the campus.Some of them are-

  • Step-Up(Fresher's dance competition)
  • Narthaki(Solo-Classical Dance competition)
  • Freshers Cult-Night
  • PAFF

Achievements

  • Here are some of our achievements-
  • 2nd Position @ Electric Heels, alcheringa 2015
  • Finalist of Channel[V] Nokia India Fest '13
  • Winners at Alcheringa ‘11 and ‘12
  • 4th position at Mood-I in December 2010
